About

Hi there! My name is Victor, I'm a software engineer and I relish all things technology (software for the most part). I currently live in Lagos, Nigeria - a beautiful city with lots to do and enough traffic to slow down lightening.

I remember it was all fun when I started out in PHP, until I decided to make a career of it all. So I switched to Java, but I eventually realised commercial programming has a way of stifling out the fun in development. Coupled with Java's rigidity, they're a match made in heaven.

In the hopes of finding flexibility and attaining true freedom, I sought after Ruby. For somebody from a Java background, Ruby felt like this. I mean what could be wrong with having several ways of doing stuff? Rails. I was inspired to seek more agile ways of juggling everything; fun with work, time with money, and coffee with doughnuts.

Time-skip to Python, everything's an object just like Ruby but without the levels of freedom and with the addition of a few rules. Why? Well because rules are necessary to protect freedom. Without rules, it is a battle of who's freedom is superior to the other. There should be a method to our madness, do as much you like within your limitations.

I created this blog to convey my thoughts via software development and hopefully connect with other humans. I am not a Python/Django sage, but don't we all have something beneficial to share.
